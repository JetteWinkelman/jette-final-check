Asten, The Netherlands

What is there to love about this lovely small town in the outskirts of Eindhoven?

Asten's Nature

Asten is very famous for its lovely nature. The most well known natural reserve being De Peel.

Het Kasteel van Asten

Close to the town border you can find the castle of Asten. This ruin is over 600 years old.

Heijligers Horse Stable

Another thing Asten is famous for is their horse stables. These stables are one of the biggest in all of the Netherlands.

In de Gloria

In de Gloria used to be a protestant church but got transformed into a venue for weddings, parties and other fun events.

Museum Klok Peel

This museum in Asten was voted most fun museum of all of Brabant.

Kerk van Asten

This is the main church of Asten, it is located in the middle of the town centre.
